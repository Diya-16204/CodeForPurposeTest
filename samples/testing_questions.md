# Sample Upload Files And Questions

Use any CSV in this folder as an upload target for the app.

## sales_data.csv

Try questions like:

- Which region generated the most revenue overall?
- How did revenue change from January to March?
- Which product or region had the biggest drop in February?
- Compare revenue and cost by month.
- Which month had the strongest margin?

## support_tickets.csv

Try questions like:

- Which channel opened the most tickets overall?
- Did resolution time improve or worsen over time?
- Which category had the most escalations?
- Compare tickets opened versus resolved by month.
- Where do we seem to have the biggest operational bottleneck?

## branch_operations.csv

Try questions like:

- Which region had the best customer satisfaction?
- Is lower wait time associated with better satisfaction?
- Which branch type handled the most transactions?
- How did complaints change over time?
- Which area showed the strongest new account growth?

## workforce_attrition.csv

Try questions like:

- Which department had the highest total exits?
- How did attrition change from 2025-Q3 to 2026-Q2?
- Which region appears most stable by headcount and exits?
- Is engagement score moving with attrition?
- Which team had the strongest hiring versus exits balance?

## Good General Prompts

- Summarize the most important trends in this dataset.
- Show me the biggest increase and biggest decrease.
- Which segment is performing best and worst?
- Give me a simple narrative and chart for the main pattern.
- Highlight any outliers or unusual changes.
